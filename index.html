About The Event

Twenzetu Bunyore

"Twenzetu Bunyore" means "Let's go to Bunyore" in Swahili. This special event is a celebration of our cultural heritage and family bonds. The Buyukah Family Visitation is an opportunity for family members from near and far to gather, reconnect, and strengthen our ties.

Bunyore, located in Vihiga County, Western Kenya, is known for its beautiful hills and rich cultural heritage. The Buyukah Residence in Essunza will serve as the perfect venue for our family gathering, providing a warm and welcoming environment surrounded by the natural beauty of the region.

This two-day event will feature storytelling, music, dance, and delicious local cuisine—rejoicing in our collective achievements and looking forward to the future of our family..

    Venue Information

    Location: The Buyukah Residence, Essunza, Bunyore, Kenya

    Facilities: Spacious outdoor gathering area, indoor meeting space, parking, restroom facilities

    Weather: Expect moderate temperatures with possible afternoon showers. Light jackets are recommended.

    Direction Tips:

    From Kisumu, take the Kisumu-Kakamega highway and turn at Luanda towards Emuhaya. Follow the signs to Essunza in Bunyore. The Buyukah Residence is marked with banners.

    What to Bring

    • Traditional attire for cultural ceremonies
    • Family photographs and memorabilia
    • Personal medications if needed
    • Walking shoes for the Bunyore Hills tour
    • A spirit of celebration and togetherness
